Introducing the Cat BMI Calculator (CBMIC)(1) (2)

CBMIC is made with the power of R…so you don't have to know anything!

Math is so confusing :(

#aargh!
bmi_of_cat <- function(circumference, legLength){
    round(((circumference/0.7062 - legLength)/0.9156) - legLength, 1) 
}

(1) BMI: Body Mass Index: a calculation of height-weight proportionality, used as a shorthand diagnosis of overweight. See https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Body_mass_index
(2) Cat BMI: the cat-specific formula for BMI. See http://www.embracepetinsurance.com/blog/measuring-my-cats-body-mass-index-the-fbmi

It's easy!
